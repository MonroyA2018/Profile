Academic Standing

Since I arrived at SJNMA I've always focus on school and cared about my academics, so that I can get good grades and get into a good college. So far it's going really good, I have very good grades, and I've also recieved awards for it, like:

Dean's List With Honors

Dean's List With Honors is awarded to those cadets that get a GPA of 3.80 or higher for the quarter. In my two years here, I've got 4 of them.

Dean's List

Dean's List is awarded to those cadets that get a GPA of 3.50 to 3.79 for the quarter. In my two years here, I've got 2 of them.

Rogers Golden Star

The Rogers Golden Star is awarded to those cadets that get a GPA of 3.80 or higher for the semester. In my two years here, I've got 3 of them.

Academy Facts

St. John's Northwestern Military Academy (SJNMA) was founded in 1884 as St. John's Military Academy (SJMA) in Delafield, Wisconsin, by Rev. Sidney T. Smythe as a private, college preparatory and leadership development school. In 1995, St. John's Military Academy merged with Northwestern Military and Naval Academy (NMNA) in Lake Geneva, Wisconsin, to become St. John's Northwestern Military Academy on the Delafield campus. SJNMA is principally a boarding school for boys and girls in grades 7-12, but also has an active day program.

Rank and Position Facts

Here at SJNMA the rank and leadership position that you held is what makes you different from other cadets, and it's the leader's job to help cadets from lower rank to teach them what their duties are

Rank

My rank is Staff Sergeant, above Sergeant and bellow Sergeant First Class. In order to get promoted to Staff Sergeant you must have at least 10 hours of community service, wait 90 days for promotion, write 1000 words essay.

Leadership Position

I'm the Bravo Company Head Squad Leader, my duty is to report to the platoon sergeant about any problem among the company and pass the word to the squad leader's to improve the company.

Sports

I've been in a lot of sports at SJNMA since last year; currently I'm in silver rifles, I've been working really hard since November and I'm finally going to the (Daytona Nationals) with the team. I hope we come back with some trophies.
